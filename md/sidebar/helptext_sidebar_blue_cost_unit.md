The costs and emissions are shown per product delivered to the target country.
You can choose to reference costs and emissions either by energy or by mass.
For energy, the units are USD/MWh and gCO₂eq/MWh, and for mass, they are USD/tonne and gCO₂eq/tonne, respectively.

If you choose _Crude steel_ or _Iron_ as final product, the costs and emissions will always be referenced by mass.
