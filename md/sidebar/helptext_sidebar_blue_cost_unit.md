The costs and emissions are shown per product delivered to the target country.
You can choose to reference costs either by energy (USD/MWh) or by mass (USD/t).

Emissions are reported in gCO₂eq/MJ for most products, regardless of the selected cost output unit.

If you select _Crude steel_ or _Iron_ as final product, the costs and emissions will always be referenced by mass (USD/t and tCO₂eq/t).
