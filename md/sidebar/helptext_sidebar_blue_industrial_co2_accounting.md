Where does your CO2 come from?
- Hard-to-abate or sustainable CO2: CO₂ comes from hard-to-abate (eg. cement) or sustainable (eg. biomass) source and does not add to the emissions balance of final product (likewise to Direct Air Capture). Costs for CO2 are assumed to be 80 USD2021/ton CO2.
- Other CO2: CO₂ comes from e.g fossil source and adds to the emissions balance of final product. Costs for CO2 are assumed to be 20 USD2021/ton CO2.
