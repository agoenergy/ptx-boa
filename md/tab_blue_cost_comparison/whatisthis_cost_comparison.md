This tab allows you to compare the calculated costs of the selected blue technology chain and product with the costs of comparable green technology chains.

These chains have the same final output product as the blue chain selected in the sidebar.
Costs for these chains are calculated for all possible supply countries, based on the following assumptions:

- Hybrid PV and Wind electricity generation
- bunker fuel for ship transport
- using a pipeline for the pair of supply and demand countries if possible
- specific costs are used for secondary CO₂ and water

In the figure, you see the median green cost, as well as the 25th and 75th percentile bands.

If _Edit input data_ is activated,  any altered input data will not be used to optimise the electrolyser and storage full load hours for the green cost calculations.
