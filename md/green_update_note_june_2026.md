Alongside the publication of the natural gas based __Low-Carbon Business Opportunity Analyser__, data was updated in the renewable based __PtX Business Opportunity Analyser__. 
The following input data categories were updated to be consistent with the Low-Carbon Business Opportunity Analyser:

- WACC data 
- USD 2025
- Updated parameters for derivative production (methanol, ammonia, efuel production and steel)

Additionally, CAPEX and OPEX were updated for the renewable energy electricity sources (RES-E). 
Furthermore, Pakistan was integrated as a new supply country.
